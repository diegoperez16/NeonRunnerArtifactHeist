SHREK (2001) - Full Screenplay
Transcribed for personal reference

FADE IN:

EXT. SWAMP - DAY

Establishing shot of a remote swamp. Murky water, gnarled trees,
fireflies at dusk. A small wooden outhouse sits at the edge of
the water.

A large green ogre SHREK emerges from the outhouse, stretching.

SHREK
(to himself, deeply satisfied)
Ahhh... that'll do.

He takes a deep breath of swamp air. This is home.

EXT. SWAMP - CONTINUOUS

Shrek goes about his morning routine: brushing his teeth with
twigs, gargling swamp water, cannonballing into the murky lake.

He is alone and he likes it that way.
